<Frame>![](/images/business.png)</Frame>

Your organization's Business profile contains the following information:

* Business type (Private, Public, Personal, Education, Government, or Non-profit)

* Legal company name

* Address where your company is registered

* Country where your company is registered

* Website and additional social media accounts

To update any of the information stored on your Business profile, follow these steps:

1. Go to **Organization settings** \> **Business profile**.

2. Edit the information as required.

3. Click **Update** to save your changes.

It's important to keep your Business profile up to date to ensure that we can provide you with accurate and efficient service.
