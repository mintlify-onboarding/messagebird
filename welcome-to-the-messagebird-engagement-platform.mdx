## What is MessageBird?

MessageBird's engagement platform is designed to streamline your communication processes, enabling you to send and receive messages across multiple channels, including [SMS](/applications/channels/sms), [Email](/applications/channels/email), and [WhatsApp](/applications/channels/whatsapp). 

With our powerful tools, you can easily automate your communication workflows, personalize your messages, and engage with your customers in real-time.

Looking for our API documentation? [It's over here](/api-reference)!
